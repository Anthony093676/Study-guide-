Study Tips

  • Time Management: Make a simple schedule and take short breaks.
  • Note-taking: Write short notes and use clear headings.
  • Avoid Distractions: Keep your phone away while studying.
  • Practice: Answer past quizzes and review your notes.
  • Stay Healthy: Sleep well and eat on time.
"Small steps every day are better than cramming at the last minute."
